# Product Overview

## Name
SCORE

## Tagline
Tu Juego. Tu Nivel. (Your Game. Your Level.)

## Subtitle
Entrena - Compite - Destaca (Train - Compete - Shine)

## Description
SCORE is a mobile-first web application for amateur soccer players featuring AI-powered video analysis, training plans, 1v1 challenges, leaderboards, events, and social community features. The platform enables players to record their skills, receive AI coaching feedback, compete in ranked challenges, and participate in local events.

## Target Audience
- Amateur soccer players looking to improve their skills
- Players wanting to compete and track rankings (city/national/friends)
- Soccer communities seeking social engagement and events

## Language
Spanish (es) - Primary user-facing language

## Core Features

### 1. AI Coach & Training
- AI-powered skill analysis via video recording
- Weekly training plans with session tracking (completed/pending/locked)
- Active challenges with point rewards
- Score metrics (AI Score out of 100)

### 2. Video Recording & Analysis
- In-app camera recording with AI active indicator
- Video upload for skill assessment
- AI Score results with metric breakdown
- Coach feedback and ranking updates

### 3. Leaderboards & Rankings
- City, national, and friends ranking tabs
- Podium view with top 3 display
- Ranking table with position tracking
- Player position tags (GK, DEF, MID, FWD)

### 4. Events
- Event discovery with skill categories
- Event detail pages with capacity and prize info
- Skill station descriptions
- Post-event results with certificates

### 5. Social & Community
- Community feed with stories rail
- Post cards with reactions (likes, comments, challenge)
- 1v1 challenge system with reputation stakes
- Player profiles with activity/challenges/events/stats tabs

### 6. Notifications & Sharing
- Notification center with unread/read states
- Shareable result cards with player stats and branding

## Current Status
Design prototype phase - 16 screens fully implemented as static UI components with no backend, authentication, or real data.
